<&|/l&>displaying other kind of dates:

<&|/l_unsafe, qq{} . loc("Query Builder") . ''&> By default RTx::Calendar display Due and Starts dates. You can select other kind of events you want with the Display Columns section in the [_1]. The following one will display the two latter, LastUpdated and an additional date from a custom field called Maintenance Date: 

  '<small>__Due__</small>',
  '<small>__Starts__</small>',
  '<small>__LastUpdated__</small>',
  '<small>__CustomField.{Maintenance Date}__</small>'
